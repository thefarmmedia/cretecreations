Choosing Your Color

What to Know Before You Decide

🔆
Lighting Changes EverythingThe same flake looks noticeably different under LED garage lighting vs. an open garage door in daylight. Always evaluate samples in your actual space.
🚗
Grey Hides Tire MarksGray and charcoal blends are most popular for a reason — they conceal rubber marks, dust, and daily wear better than warm or light tones.
🎨
Custom Blends AvailableIf none of the 27 standard blends are exactly right, Arturo can create a custom or hybrid blend at an upcharge. Ask at the free consultation.
📐
Full Coverage — Wall to WallEvery Crete Creations installation is 100% full broadcast flake — wall to wall, no bare spots. The color you see in the sample is the color you get everywhere.
