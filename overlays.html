Concrete Overlays — Transform Without Replacing

Decorative concrete overlays are one of the most versatile and cost-effective tools in Arturo Escalante's concrete artistry arsenal. Rather than tearing out and replacing existing concrete, a properly applied overlay creates an entirely new surface that can take virtually any form — wood planks, stone patterns, tile looks, acid stain compositions, custom artistic designs, or any combination.

With 24+ years of overlay installation experience, Arturo's skill goes well beyond what most regional contractors can offer. Our wood-look overlays achieve such realistic plank patterns and grain texture that visitors often can't tell they're standing on concrete.

Interior Overlay Systems

Wood-Look Concrete Overlays

Realistic plank patterns with custom widths, authentic grain texture, layered stain coats for wood-tone depth and variation. Available in dozens of wood tone colorways from light ash to dark walnut. No warping, no refinishing, no water damage — looks like hardwood, performs like concrete.

Stone and Slate Patterns

Flagstone, slate, and natural stone patterns applied over concrete through combination of scoring, texture application, and layered staining. Beautiful on interior floors and covered outdoor spaces.

Tile and Geometric Patterns

Large-format tile, Spanish tile, Saltillo, geometric patterns — precision scored and colored to create grout lines and tile boundaries indistinguishable from the real thing.

Acid Stain and Concrete Dye

Reactive acid stains create rich, translucent color permanently bonded into the concrete itself — not a coating sitting on top. Every acid-stained floor is unique because every concrete slab has different mineral composition. The mottled, organic variation creates floors with a natural depth that painted or coated surfaces cannot replicate.
