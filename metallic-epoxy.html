Metallic Epoxy Floors

Every metallic pour Arturo creates is completely unique — no two floors are ever the same. Liquid art that hardens into a permanent, durable surface.

When Your Floor Becomes a Work of Art

Metallic epoxy floors are something else entirely from standard flake or solid color systems. It's part functional floor coating, part liquid art — a system where color, chemistry, and Arturo's 24 years of technique combine to create a one-of-a-kind surface that cannot be replicated on any other floor. Ever.

When metallic pigments are introduced into liquid epoxy, they continue to move and interact as the epoxy cures, creating organic, flowing patterns — molten metal, ocean waves, cosmic formations, pearlescent marble. Arturo can guide and influence the movement, but the final result always has an element that no human hand can perfectly control. That's why every metallic floor is genuinely unique.

Available Effects

  • Lava flow patterns — dark channels and bright metallic rivers, like cooling volcanic rock
  • Ocean and water effects — flowing, wave-like movement across the entire surface
  • Nebula / cosmic patterns — multi-color interactions creating deep, layered visual complexity
  • Pearl and marble effects — soft, elegant movement with high-contrast veining
  • Custom compositions — Arturo works with you to develop a specific color story for your space

Color Options

Gold, silver, copper, bronze, oceanic blues, deep greens, rich reds and burgundies, purple, white and pearl, gunmetal and charcoal — available individually or in multi-color combinations that flow and interact across the floor. Arturo brings photos of past metallic work to every consultation so you can see what's possible before committing.

Performance

As extraordinary as metallic floors look, they perform just as well as our standard polyaspartic floor systems. The metallic layer is sandwiched between the concrete surface and a commercial-grade polyaspartic topcoat — the same UV stable, chemical resistant, durable finish as any Crete Creations floor. Beauty and performance, together.
