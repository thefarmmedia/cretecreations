Transform your existing driveway without tearing it out. 11 overlay styles — acid stain, brick inlay, stone scoring, stenciled patterns, custom medallions. Real Arturo projects.

The Smart Alternative to Replacing Your Driveway

Concrete driveways don't age gracefully on their own. Missouri's freeze-thaw cycles crack them. UV fades and discolors them. Oil stains, tire marks, and decades of vehicle traffic leave them looking worn. The traditional answer — full demolition and replacement — is expensive, disruptive, and produces plain concrete again. Today, there's a dramatically better option.

Spray-on concrete overlay systems bond directly to your existing driveway, create a fresh new surface, and can be finished in virtually any design style — from simple solid stain to elaborate brick inlay grids, custom stone scoring, stenciled tile patterns, and hand-cut decorative medallions. The result looks like brand-new, custom-designed concrete at 40–60% of replacement cost.

Overlay vs. Replacement

Cost: typically 40–60% less. Time: 2–3 days vs. weeks for demo and pour. Disruption: minimal. Design options: vastly more with an overlay. If your slab is structurally sound, overlay is almost always the right call. Arturo assesses this honestly during the free consultation.
