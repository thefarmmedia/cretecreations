Whether you need a garage floor coating, shop floor, basement floor, commercial floor, metallic epoxy system, or decorative concrete coating, this calculator gives you a fast starting point before scheduling an on-site estimate.

Concrete prep matters.

At Crete Creations LLC, every project starts with proper concrete preparation. The surface is mechanically diamond ground to remove contaminants, open the slab, and create the right profile for the coating system to bond. Cracks, pitting, and damaged areas are addressed before installation so the finished floor looks clean and performs the way it should.
