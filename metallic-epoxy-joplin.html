Metallic Epoxy Floors
Joplin, Missouri

Arturo Escalante · 24+ years · (417) 483-7082 · Free on-site quote · Use the instant calculator for a ballpark first.

Get Instant Estimate → 📞 (417) 483-7082

Metallic epoxy floors are among the most distinctive flooring options available — and several of Arturo's most recognized Joplin projects are metallic pours. Restaurants near Main Street, luxury garages in south Joplin, commercial showrooms throughout the area. Every installation is genuinely unique because metallic pigments move and interact as the epoxy cures. Learn more about metallic epoxy floors →

Metallic epoxy is priced at $12–$16 per square foot — use the instant calculator and select "Metallic Epoxy System" for a personalized estimate. The same complete 4-layer system applies: diamond grinding, moisture vapor barrier, metallic epoxy pour, and UV-stable polyaspartic topcoat.

Color options range from classic silver and gold to dramatic ocean blues, deep reds, and fully custom multi-color pours. Arturo brings reference photos of real projects to every consultation so you can see actual results before committing.
