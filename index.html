About Crete Creations LLC

One Name.
One Standard.

Arturo Escalante founded Crete Creations LLC with a simple principle: every concrete surface deserves master-level care and the right product for the job. Based in Joplin, Missouri — with 24+ years of experience working with epoxy, polyurea, and polyaspartic systems.

💧
Moisture Vapor BarrierEvery installation. Protects your floor from moisture-driven failure from below — the hidden force most contractors ignore.

Our Process

How We Do It
Right.

01
Free Consultation

Arturo visits your site, assesses the concrete, evaluates moisture levels, and determines the right system. Free, no obligation.

02
Diamond Grinding & Prep

Industrial diamond grinding profiles every square foot. Crack repair. Moisture vapor barrier installed. Where most contractors cut corners — we don't.

03
System Application

100% solids epoxy base coat, 100% full flake broadcast, grout coat, and high-solids polyaspartic topcoat — applied with 24 years of precision.

04
Final Walkthrough

Arturo walks the finished floor with you, reviews care and maintenance, and doesn't leave until you're completely satisfied.
