About Crete Creations LLC

One Name.
One Standard.

Arturo Escalante founded Crete Creations LLC with a simple principle: every concrete surface deserves master-level care and the right product for the job. Based in Joplin, Missouri — with 24+ years of experience working with epoxy, polyurea, and polyaspartic systems — Arturo specifies the system that best fits your specific concrete, not a one-size-fits-all package pushed on every floor regardless of conditions.

The result is a body of work that spans residential garage floors, one-of-a-kind metallic epoxy pours, elaborate driveway overlay systems, large-scale commercial floors, and custom outdoor concrete — all executed personally by Arturo on every project.

Our Process

How We Do It Right.

01
Free Consultation

Arturo visits your site, assesses the concrete, evaluates moisture levels, and determines the right system. Brings physical color sample chips. Free, no obligation.

02
Diamond Grinding & Prep

Industrial diamond grinding profiles every square foot. Crack repair. Moisture vapor barrier installed. Where most contractors cut corners — we never do.

03
System Application

100% solids epoxy base coat, 100% full flake broadcast wall to wall, grout coat, and high-solids polyaspartic topcoat — applied with 24 years of precision.

04
Final Walkthrough

Arturo walks the finished floor with you personally, explains care and maintenance, and doesn't leave until you're completely satisfied. Walk-on ready in 24 hours.
